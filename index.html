WE ARE VALORANT

DEFY THE LIMITS

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to
attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life
per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on
foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

YOUR AGENTS

CREATIVITY IS YOUR GREATEST WEAPON.

More than guns and bullets, you’ll choose an Agent armed with adaptive,
swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine.
No two Agents play alike, just as no two highlight reels will look the same.

YOUR Maps

FIGHT AROUND THE WORLD

Each map is a playground to showcase your creative thinking.
Purpose-built for team strategies, spectacular plays, and clutch
moments. Make the play others will imitate for years to come..
